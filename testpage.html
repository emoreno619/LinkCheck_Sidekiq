        Praise the Lord, hallelujah!

        I haven't gone to church in a long time, but I sing praises when it is warranted. Fat Angel makes me sing from the heart.

        This sweet, angelic spot exudes upscale lodge with a casual chic feel: marble bar, pew style booth seating and a handsome wall holding some crafted brews and carefully chosen vino. Don't mind the imposing Moosehead hanging above, Rocky and Bullwinkle totally approve.

        Fat Angel seamlessly blends fantastic wine bar food with excellent tipples. The menu is 90% alcohol (as it should be!) beers are arranged according to your taste: light, crisp, heavy, dark. Same for the wines and for those who aren't feeling both, they offer several aperitifs as well.

        It felt a little stuffy inside because of the rain, otherwise, I can imagine sitting here for hours on a warm SF night with the bay windows wide open. A crisp and zesty Picpoul was affordably priced at $8/glass.  My gf loves Riesling and this one from Kabinett is a richer style with sweetness in the end.  Of course, you can't go wrong with a Hefeweizen (Ayinger) or a dark stout like Double Chocolate Stout from England or a breakfast stout from Mikeller Bar (the Danes KNOW beer!)

        All this imbibing made us hungry: we devoured an excellent meat board with speck, salami and duck terrine. On the board were also FLAVORED BUTTERS like maple bacon and Philz coffee. You wouldn't be able to resist spreading these on toast! The flatbread pizzas are pleasingly light, even with rich toppings such as gorgonzola, prosciutto and grapes on it. The chicken pot pie had a really flaky crust, but I didn't taste anything special about the tarragon cream sauce.

        Hark the herald angels sing and glory to ... the genius who brought Fat Angel to San Francisco. Let's say Amen to that!

      Specialties

      30 wines by the glass from around the globe, 6 California draft wines & 9 artisan draft beers, and a revolving, eclectic 100+ bottle beer menu.  Our food is American comfort with mac n' cheese, chicken pot pie, beef sliders, huge salads and flatbreads.  Our ever revolving meat, cheese, and butter boards are not to be missed.

      History

      Established in 2010.

      Fat Angel was born out of a passion for The Fillmore District. We are a casual wine bar and cafe that serves comforting food and drink to the community. We offer a handpicked, eclectic mix of wine and beer that spans the globe, focusing on regions and producers that offer exceptional value and quality. Our restaurant was built with reclaimed, repurposed, and recycled materials from around San Francisco. This creates an intimate, old world environment, perfect for hanging out with friends, drinking a glass of wine, and eating a nourishing meal. Fat Angel's mission is to become The Fillmore's benchmark in food, service, price, & atmosphere.
